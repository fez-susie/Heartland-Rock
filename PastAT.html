Past Assignments

The other two assignments for this subject included an issues exploration and a skills development. Both of these have been incorperated into Heartland Rock.

Use of Arrays in Video Games

AT-1: Skills Development

For the skills development, I looked into arrays and how they work. Simmilarly to a mathematical matrix, an array stores information by giving catagorical data a numerical value. Practical knowledge around functions such as "for loops" were developed in this skills development too.

Gender Representation in Video Games

AT-3: Issues Exploration

For the issues exploration, I looked into the gender equality and representation in games. After searching through some papers which showed nasty and horrible statistics, it was obvious to me that there remained an issue in the industry. Some solutions were identified and one of them was included into the Heartland Rock as a way to show the application of the solutions.
